Dare 4 Good

Dare. Friends. Fun. Social Good.

About Dare 4 Good

Dare 4 Good is a light-hearted social network app focused around encouraging social good through small random acts of kindness. Many people are unaware of issues going on in the world, and this app not only tries to educate and inform everyone on various issues around them but also encourages people to go out and help their community and the world.

Challenge friends to carry out randomly generated challenges with a single tap.

Every challenge comes with an interesting fact and a link to learn more about a specific issue.

Who Is It For?

This app is for those who like to compete with friends. Who want to learn more about issues such as homelessness, poverty, global warming, etc. Who want to infuse small acts of kindness as habits into their daily lives.

Who Would Use It?

Lots of millenials and young teens would this product. Teens love fun/games and competing with their friends, which adds an element unlike other social network apps out there.
